.# Privacy Policy for SoulPure

**Last Updated: April 5, 2026**

SoulPure ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
SoulPure is a **"Local-First"** application. 
* **Personal Data:** We **do not collect, store, or share** any personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, or location.
* **Device Data:** The app does not access your contacts, camera, or microphone.

### 2. Third-Party Services (Advertising)
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
* For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### 3. Data Security
Since no personal data is collected or transmitted to our servers, your data remains secure on your local device.
