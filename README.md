# Privacy Policy for SoulPure
**Last Updated: October 24, 2024**

### 1. Information Collection and Use
SoulPure is a **"Local-First"** application.
* **Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or share any PII (name, email, or location).
* **Notifications:** We use the `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` permission strictly for daily quotes.
* **Local Storage:** Favorites and settings are stored only on your device.

### 2. Third-Party Services (Advertising)
We use **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve ads.
* [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

### 3. Children’s Privacy
SoulPure does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Our content is family-friendly.

### 4. Data Security
No data is transmitted to our servers; it remains secure on your local device.
